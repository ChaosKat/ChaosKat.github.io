My Inspiration

I'm not someone who's ever been inclined to idolize people. I have very little interest in celebrity culture, can reconize exactly one actor, and even with media that is tied to a figure I'm rarely attached to the person more than the content, and have little loyalty to their creations because of this. Due to this the people I do tend to idolize are people in my own life rather than public figures. However, I have allowed myself to get attached and follow a few select creators, primarily indie game creators, one of which is Toby Fox

Toby Fox is most famous for creating the hit indie game Undertale and to a lesser but still large extent its sequel Deltarune which is still in development.
