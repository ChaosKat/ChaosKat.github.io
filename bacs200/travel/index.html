Fun Activities!

Learn the Art of Fishing

Shrimp Island has a long history of fishers. Learn from masters in the field on how large scale fishing is conducted, and get the opprotunity to try it yourself!

Eat Fresh Seafood

The resturants on Shrimp Island get their seafood directly from the islands ocean fresh everyday. Don't miss the opprotunity to try some of the freshest seafood around!

Beautiful Plantlife

The residents of Shrimp Island live in harmony with our planet, which shows in the incredible wildlife that has grown around the island. See rare flowers, forests of palm trees, and a sky full of stars every night! Shrimp Island provides many unique opprotunities for the photographer in us all!
