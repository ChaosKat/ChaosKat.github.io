Detailed Information About Vacation Packages

All Packages

The cost fluctuation shown on the website is meant to represent price differences depending on the cost of airport transportation. The $200 fluctuation accounts for distance away from Shrimp Island in the US, with Florida being the cheapest and Washington being the most expensive.

Departure Incorperated operates solely within the US, however Alaska flights are not supported. If you want to work with us within another country or Alaska than you must fly to a/another US state using your own resources. Additional support can be found on our companies FAQ page.

Shrimp Island packages are provided through a deal between Departure Incorperated and the residents of Shrimp Island. As such the residents should be respected, and non-participating resturants and businesses should be respected and ignored. You will be fined if you are deemed to be a nusance to the locals or other tourists.

Trips to Shrimp Island can only be booked during Spring, and only fly during Summer. If any cancelation happens between these times you will recieve a full refund.

You may book for multiple people at a time when planning a trip. Each additional person will recieve a 20% discount on their trip fee.

Basic Package

Your meal plan allows for eight meals per tourist across the four days. Remaining meal plans will not be refunded.

The basic plan contains no transportation options besides flight. If you would like transportation, please check out the transport package instead.

Each tourist will be given a small hotel room with a single bed, bathroom, and various storage options.

Tour Package

The tour guides are employeed by Destination Incorperated, and paid 15 dollars an hour.

Tour guides are allowed a good amount of freedom in giving tours, although some places are required visits. If you feel your tour guide is taking you to uncomfortable or unsavory places please contact us.

The tour guides are locals on the island, often indigenous peoples. Please treat them with respect. If you are found to not be treating them with proper respect, you will be subject to a fine and be banned from Destination Incorperated tours in the future.

Transport Package

The avaliable rental transport options are - car, bike, scooter, skateboard, and a rental boat for Shrimp Beach.

Cost of gas is not included in payment and must be paid for by the tourist.

As Destination Incorperated rents its vehicles through other rental companies, any concerns regarding rental vehicles must be settled with the rental company itself.

Destination Incorperated is not responsible for any death or injury that occurs as a result of rental vehicles. Only the rental company can be held accountable.

Deluxe Package

The hotel rooms in the deluxe package include a single bed, bathroom, kitchen, TV, living space, and various storage options.

If you would like to exclude the guided tour from your deluxe package please check the box that says "No guided tour" on the checkout page. You will recieve a $150 discount.

The pass to Shrimp Beach lasts four days, either from the day you arrive to the day before you leave, or the day after you arrive to the day you leave, depending on which day is spent at Shrimp Island the longest.
